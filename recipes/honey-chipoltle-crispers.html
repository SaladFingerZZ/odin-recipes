Honey Chipoltle Chicken Crispers

Chilis Copycat Chicken Crispers

I dont know about you but I love me some chilis chicken crispers! The sauced, juicy, breaded goodness is to die for. Always has been my absolute favorite thing. Today I am going to teach you how you can make chilis famous honey chipoltle chicken cripsers at home.

Ingredients

    Sauce

  • 2/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp ground chipoltle
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 6-10 cups vegtable oil

    Batter

  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour

    Breading

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 10-12 chicken tenderloins

Instructions

  1. Combine sauce ingredients in a small saucepan. on medium, heat until boiling. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes, remove from heat.
  2. Heat oil in fryer to 350 degrees F.
  3. Whisk together all batter ingredients excluding flour, in a shallow bowl. Continue whisking about 30 seconds to dissolve salt. Whisk in flour.
  4. Make the breading by combining all ingrediends in another shallow bowl.
  5. Dip a piece of chicken in the wet batter, coat and let some of the excess drip off.
  6. Dip the now battered piece of chicken into the dry breading. Make sure to coat the whole tender.
  7. Place the battered and breaded chicken into the oil, fry for 4 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. When chicken is finished frying, place onto paper towels while the others fry.
  9. When all the chicken is done, place them into a large bowl. Pour in the sauce and toss until all chicken is well coated
