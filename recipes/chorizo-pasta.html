Cheesy Chorizo Pasta

Its slap yo daddy good!

This meal comes together very quickly. Sometimes you just dont feel like cooking after a long day. This is one of those that wont feel like another chore when you just want to kick back and enjoy the night. Its hard to beat a simple delicous pasta!

Ingredients

  • 2 links of chorizo, casing removed
  • 1/4 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 small zuchini, sliced and quartered
  • 6 oz dried pasta
  • Hot chicken or beef stock, water works too just enough to cover pasta
  • Salt to taste
  • 50 g milk + 5 g cornstarch
  • 2 slices american cheese
  • 28 g white sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • 28 g chipoltle gouda, grated regular gouda works too
  • Cilantro to garnish

Instructions

  1. Place stock(or water) in a pot on medium-high heat or warm in the microwave.
  2. Slice the onion and quarter the zuchini.
  3. Set a pan over medium-high heat and add chorizo. Break apart with spatula until ground chorizo is formed, cook until done. Transfer into a bowl, keep as much fat in the pan as possible.
  4. Add the onions and zuchini to the pan, cook for 3 minutes stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove and add the onions and zuchini into the same bowl as the chorizo.
  6. Add dried pasta to pot, cover with just enough stock or water to cover the pasta. Bring to a boil.
  7. Cook until pasta is al dente, add more liquid as needed if it reduces too quickly. When pasta is finished the stock should be significantly reduced into a sauce like consistency.
  8. Turn the heat to low. Add the milk and cornstarch slurry. Stir in until the sauce comes together. Stir in the cheeses until thickened to your liking.
  9. Add the chorizo and vegtable mixture, stir to combine. Serve with cilantro on top.
